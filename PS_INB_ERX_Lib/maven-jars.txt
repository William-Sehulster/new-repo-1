
The vistalink related jars are third party and should be installed/deployed in your local maven repo.

1-  Copy the jar files  in your project under PS_INB_ERX_Lib, for example C:\Data\Inbound_eRx_V4\PS_INB_ERX_Lib

2 - Go the jar file folder and run one by one each command.


2a-
C:\Data\Inbound_eRx_V4\PS_INB_ERX_Lib\mavenrepo\gov\va\med\vistalink\vljConnector\1.6.1.010


mvn deploy:deploy-file -Dfile=vljConnector-1.6.1.010.jar  -Durl=file://C://Data//MavenRepo -DgroupId=gov.va.med.vistalink -DartifactId=vljConnector -Dversion=1.6.1.010 -Dpackaging=jar


2b-
C:\Data\Inbound_eRx_V4\PS_INB_ERX_Lib\mavenrepo\gov\va\med\vistalink\vljFoundationsLib\1.6.1.010


mvn deploy:deploy-file -Dfile=vljFoundationsLib-1.6.1.010.jar -Durl=file://C://Data//MavenRepo  -DgroupId=gov.va.med.vistalink -DartifactId=vljFoundationsLib -Dversion=1.6.1.010 -Dpackaging=jar


2c-


C:\Data\Inbound_eRx_V4\PS_INB_ERX_Lib\mavenrepo\gov\va\med\vistalink\vljSecurity\1.6.1.010
